A Home for Every Bihari & Jharkhandi in South Carolina

BJSC is a vibrant community of families and friends from Bihar and Jharkhand who now call South Carolina home. We come together to celebrate festivals, preserve our rich cultural heritage, and support one another as an extended family.

Through cultural programs, social gatherings, and community service, we create a welcoming space where Indian roots and American life blend beautifully.

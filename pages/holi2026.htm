Holi 2026

Event Details

The Bihar Jharkhand group of South Carolina (BJSC) warmly invites you to celebrate Holi! This is a private event exclusive to the BJSC community. To keep our gathering personal, no outside guests are allowed.

Close/immediate family members of active members may attend as an exception, provided the guest fee is paid for each attendee.
